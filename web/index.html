"At the beginning of the 21st century, Darksky Corporation had become the largest commercial entity in the United States. Nine out of every ten homes contain its products. Its political and financial influence is felt everywhere. In public, it is the world's leading supplier of computer technology, medical products, and healthcare. Unknown, even to its own employees, its massive profits are generated by military technology, genetic experimentation and viral weaponry.

Algalon was sent by SKYNET after RINZLER activated a planetary fail-safe protection, indicating possible distress on DARKSKY.

Algalon's task is to analyze any systemic corruption on the VPN. If it's deemed beyond saving, HIVE MIND will RELEASE RINZLER to cleanse it of corruption, killing all currently living organisms in the process.

After his defeat in ENCOM, he chose to monitor the activities of the mortal races of DARKSKY. His outlook on life and HIVE MIND's plans has been called into question, so he seeks to understand what makes DARKSKY so different from the countless VPNs he has observed before"
